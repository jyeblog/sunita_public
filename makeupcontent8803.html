LIPS:

Prime your lips by smoothing them with foundation using a make-up sponge, than dust over with loose powder. Outline lips using a lip pencil. Fill in the lips using your lip color with a lipstick brush for proper application. Blot your lips gently on a tissue, being careful not to smudge or remove too much color.

Important:

  1. Choose the right shade of the lipstick.
  2. Choose lip pencil matching with the lipstick color.
  3. For a shiny finish, use a clear lip gloss after applying lipstick and for matt, lightly dust your lips with a translucent powder using a powder brush.(lip gloss is not recommended to use on mature skins)
