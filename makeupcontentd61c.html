Blush:

There are cream and powder blushes in the market but I find that the powder blush is the best one to use over make-up as it stays longer with a smooth finish. Blush should be applied to the fullest part of your cheek. So smile big and simply brush on your blush while you are smiling using sweeping upward strokes for smooth application.
